LEARN ABOUT DONATION

Blood donation is the process of voluntarily giving blood, either whole blood or specific components of blood, such as platelets or plasma, for transfusion to another person. Blood donation is an essential aspect of healthcare as it plays a vital role in saving millions of lives worldwide. The blood donation process typically involves several steps, including registration, a medical screening, the actual donation process, and a brief recovery period. The screening process ensures that the blood is safe for transfusion and that the donor is healthy enough to donate. After donation, the blood is tested for various infectious diseases before it can be used for transfusion.
